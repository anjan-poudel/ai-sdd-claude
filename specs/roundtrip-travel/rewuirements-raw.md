
Think very deeply about how current Tours and Activities ( experiences) market can be disrupted or how a small player can provide some unique value propositions to customers.
For instance, almost all OTAs purely sell products. Their other content ( destination/attraction )  pages are designed for SEO and to funnel traffic to their order funnels.
One value proposition will be to make bookable products just one part of a platform website and provide lots of free content and highly tailored local content and attractions and things to do for family, singles and everyone in-between - filterable by age group and family dynamics etc.
The site can remember a user profile and then make it easy for them to browse and navigate the site to find things they are looking for.
In short: the vision is for the site to be a one stop shop for travellers looking for exepriences and 'things to do' in destinations.

Instead of starting broad, I want to start a but narrow and target - **Road Trips**.
I want to start off with a website that is tailored for providing information about places and things to do on the way to and back on a road trip.
For example,Judging by the distance and every 2 hours stoppage to revive, suggestions can be made to tailor people's stoppage routine -which means
perhaps finding towns and natural attractions every two hours for rest and sight seeing.
If travelling with children, the 2 hrs stoppage becomes even more important and the site/app should find stoppage areas with playground and toilet/parent-room.
There are a lot of such tailored services that can be provided depending on customer demographics, where and how far they are travelling etc.
The site will show a mix of free and paid things to do and attractions - but will try to find the cheapest or best tickets from other OTAs like Viator and GetYourGuide.

what will the site do?
