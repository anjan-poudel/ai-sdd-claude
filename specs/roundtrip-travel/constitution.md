# Constitution

## Project Purpose

**RoadTrip Experiences Platform** — A road-trip-first travel experiences platform that disrupts the OTA (Online Travel Agency) market by being a genuine one-stop-shop for travellers, not just a product sales funnel.

### Vision
Most OTAs purely sell products; their content pages exist only to funnel SEO traffic into booking flows. This platform's differentiator is that **bookable products are just one part of a richer platform** — with free content, highly tailored local attractions and things-to-do, filterable by demographics (family, singles, age groups, children's needs), and a personalised user profile that learns traveller preferences over time.

### Phase 1 Focus: Road Trips
- **Road-trip itinerary builder**: given origin, destination, and travel party, generate a recommended route with stopovers every ~2 hours
- **Stopover intelligence**: surface towns, natural attractions, rest areas, playgrounds, parent rooms, and toilets calibrated to party demographics
- **POI indexing**: aggregate Points of Interest from government sources (national parks, rest areas, playgrounds), open data (OpenStreetMap / Google-led consortia), and scraped sources
- **Mixed free/paid content**: show free attractions alongside bookable products; source cheapest/best tickets via Viator and GetYourGuide affiliate links
- **Revenue model**: affiliate commissions (Viator, GetYourGuide) + display advertising

### Phase 2 (future)
Direct API connectivity with suppliers and reservation platforms (bypass OTA middlemen, ~30% margin improvement)

### Phase 3 (future)
Open aggregator platform with ML-powered deduplication and clustering; white-label API partners

## Core Domain Entities

| Entity | Description |
|---|---|
| `Destination` | A geographic place (city, region, national park) with curated content and SEO pages |
| `Attraction` | A free or paid POI — physical location with opening hours, categories, demographics tags |
| `Product` | A bookable experience (tour, activity, ticket) with variants, pricing, and OTA affiliate links |
| `Itinerary` | A user-generated road trip plan: route, stopovers, attraction/product slots |
| `UserProfile` | Saved traveller demographics, preferences, and past itineraries |
